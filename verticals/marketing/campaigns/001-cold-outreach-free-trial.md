## A/B TEST STRATEGY

### What We're Testing
**Version A — "Pain Point" (Problem-Aware)**
- Leads with the prospect's specific pain point
- Subject line calls out missed calls / lost revenue directly
- Opens with a stat or frustration they recognize
- CTA: Start free trial to stop the bleeding

**Version B — "Curiosity / Social Proof" (Outcome-Curious)**
- Leads with a result from a business like theirs
- Subject line teases an intriguing outcome without naming RunBy
- Opens with a mini story or surprising number
- CTA: See how they did it → free trial

### What We're Measuring
| Metric | Target | Winner Criteria |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Open Rate | >25% | Higher open rate wins subject line test |
| Click Rate | >5% | Higher click rate wins body/CTA test |
| Trial Signup | >2% | Higher conversion wins overall |
| Reply Rate | >3% | Higher reply = better engagement signal |

### Test Duration
Run for 7 days or until 200+ sends per version (whichever comes first), then declare winner and scale.
