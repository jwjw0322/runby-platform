About RunBy

We believe small business owners should run their business — not be run by it.

RunBy gives service businesses AI-powered staff that handles operations 24/7, so owners can focus on the work that actually grows revenue.

RunBy started with a simple observation: the best tradespeople and service business owners in the country are spending half their time on work that doesn't require their expertise. Answering phones. Confirming appointments. Chasing invoices. Sending follow-ups. Managing schedules. Responding to reviews.

These are all critical tasks. But none of them require a licensed electrician, a skilled HVAC technician, or a med spa owner with 15 years of experience. They require reliable, tireless staff — and that's exactly what most small businesses can't afford.

A full-time receptionist costs $40-55K per year and only works 40 hours a week. For a business doing $300K-$1M in revenue, that's a massive line item for someone who still can't answer the phone at 9 PM on a Tuesday when a homeowner's AC goes out.

RunBy is the alternative. AI-powered staff that handles calls, bookings, follow-ups, invoicing, reviews, and daily operations — 24/7, trained on your specific business, for a fraction of what you'd pay one employee.

We're not a chatbot. We're not a call answering service. We're a full operational layer for your business that gets smarter every week.
