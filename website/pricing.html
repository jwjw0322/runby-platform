Common Questions

How does the 14-day free trial work?

Start any plan without a credit card. You get full access to all features in your tier for 14 days. At the end of the trial, you'll be prompted to enter payment to continue. No surprise charges.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade anytime from your dashboard. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-cycle, you'll be prorated for the difference.

What happens if I go over my call limit?

We'll never cut off your AI mid-conversation. If you're consistently exceeding your plan, we'll reach out to recommend an upgrade. There are no surprise overage charges.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No long-term contracts. Monthly plans are month-to-month — cancel anytime. Annual plans are billed yearly at a 20% discount, and you can cancel before renewal.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) through Stripe. ACH bank transfers are available for annual plans.

Do I need to set anything up myself?

Nope. After signup, we schedule a 30-minute onboarding call where we learn about your business. We handle the entire AI configuration. You'll be live within 48 hours.
