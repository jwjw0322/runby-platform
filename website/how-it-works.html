How It Works

Live in 48 hours. No IT department required.

From signup to a fully operational AI employee — here's exactly what happens, step by step.

01

Sign Up & Choose Your Plan

Day 1 — 5 minutes

Pick the plan that fits your business. Every plan starts with a 14-day free trial — no credit card required. You'll get immediate access to your dashboard and a confirmation email with next steps.

02

Onboarding Call

Day 1-2 — 30 minutes

We schedule a quick call to learn everything about your business. This is the only thing we need from you. After this, we handle the rest.

Services & Pricing

What you offer, your service area, and how you price jobs.

Hours & Availability

Your business hours, how you handle after-hours, and emergency protocols.

Tone & Personality

How you want your AI staff to sound — friendly, professional, casual, bilingual, etc.

Tools & Integrations

Your calendar, CRM, invoicing tools, and any other systems RunBy needs to connect with.

03

We Build Your AI Employee

Day 1-2 — Behind the scenes

Our team configures your RunBy agent based on everything from the onboarding call. This includes setting up the voice persona, training it on your specific services and FAQ, connecting your calendar and tools, and building custom workflows for your business type.

We test everything internally before it ever touches a real customer call. Your AI employee will know your business as well as your best front-desk person.

04

Go Live

Day 2 — Flip the switch

We forward your business phone to RunBy (or give you a new dedicated number). From that moment, your AI employee is live — answering calls, booking appointments, sending confirmations, and handling customer inquiries 24/7.

You'll get your first daily briefing the next morning: a clean summary of what happened, what's scheduled, and what needs your attention.

05

It Gets Smarter Every Week

Ongoing — Automatic

RunBy learns from every interaction. It picks up on your customers' patterns, your most common questions, seasonal trends, and how your business operates. The longer it runs, the better it gets — no manual retraining needed.

You'll also get weekly performance reports showing calls handled, appointments booked, revenue captured, and follow-ups completed.

Day-to-Day Operations

Everything your AI employee handles.

Once live, here's what RunBy takes off your plate — automatically, 24/7, without you lifting a finger.

Inbound Calls & Texts

Answers every call and message instantly. Asks the right questions, provides service info, and routes urgent issues to you.

Appointment Booking

Checks your real-time availability, books the job, sends the customer a confirmation text, and adds it to your calendar.

Reminders & Confirmations

Day-before reminders, 30-minute heads-up texts, and automatic rescheduling if someone needs to move their appointment.

Follow-Up Sequences

Post-service check-ins, review requests, rebooking prompts for repeat customers, and win-back campaigns for lapsed clients.

Invoice Collection

Polite, persistent payment reminders integrated with your invoicing system. Get paid faster without the awkward conversations.

Review Management

Requests reviews from satisfied customers. Responds to Google and Yelp reviews. Alerts you immediately to negative feedback.

Daily Owner Briefing

Every morning: yesterday's activity, today's schedule, outstanding items, and the few things that actually need your attention.

24/7 Coverage

Nights, weekends, holidays — RunBy never sleeps. Emergencies get escalated. Everything else gets handled and logged for you.
